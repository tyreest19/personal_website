About

Hello, My name is Tyree Stevenson and I am a Sophomore Computer Science Major from Cleveland, Ohio. I had a pretty rough high school experience I was caught up trying to find out where I fit socially and during that process, my grades began to suffer. However, during my senior year of high school things started to change for the better. In October, a Morehouse recruiter named Shawn Crosby visited my school. Mr.Crosby was a Morehouse Alum and also an alum of my high school. After speaking with Crosby, I became immediately interested in Morehouse. While attending the HBCU college fair, I met two Morehouse men Jonathan Tyes and Marques Martin. After having breakfast, with them I became completed sold on Morehouse. By a wing and a prayer, I was able to attend Morehouse the following school year. During my first semester at Morehouse, I worked extremely hard to prove to myself and my family that I did not waste this opportunity. I completed my Freshman year an 3.94 GPA and a summer internship with Google. My internship with Google was amazing I was able to live in Seattle for a summer and visit California several times. Exposing me to experiences and opportunities which I thought I would never achieve. Lastly, Morehouse has afforded me opportunities that I could never imagine and I cannot wait to see what else my future will have in store.
